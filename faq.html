What should I wear?

Dressy Casual. Wear something you'd be comfortable in for a spring evening in the gardens

Can I bring a date or plus one?

Due to limited space, we can only accommodate guests whose names appear on the invitation. Please check your RSVP for the names included in your party.

Is the venue indoors or outdoors?

The reception will be at the Grisham Pavilion in Huntsville Botanical Gardens, which is a covered outdoor space. We recommend dressing for a spring evening—it may be warm, so light layers are a good idea.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes! Free parking is available at the Huntsville Botanical Gardens. Follow signs to the Grisham Pavilion parking area.

Are children welcome?

Yes! Your children are more than welcome to join! Please text us and let us know if you plan to bring your kiddos, so we can get the correct head count!

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive by 4:00 PM. The celebration will run until 7:00 PM.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes! We'll have food and drinks available throughout the reception.
